A Decentralized Protocol for Global Sports Gaming. Built on Cardano.

1. Abstract

SportsBeep is a decentralized fantasy sports platform designed to solve the issues of trust, liquidity, and slow payouts in the traditional gaming industry. By leveraging Cardano's eUTXO model and Plutus smart contracts, we provide a transparent, provably fair environment where players truly own their assets and compete in a global liquid market.

2. The Problem & Market Opportunity

Traditional Daily Fantasy Sports (DFS) platforms suffer from high rake fees, regional restrictions, and opaque scoring systems. Furthermore, users do not "own" their achievements or history; they are merely entries in a private database.

3. Core Ecosystem Mechanics

On-Chain Verification

All game entries and prize distributions are executed via smart contracts, removing human intervention and ensuring total fairness.

Dynamic NFT Player Cards

Unlock, trade, and upgrade player cards that store historical performance data and provide gameplay boosts.

Community Governance

$BEEP holders influence game rules, scoring multipliers, and treasury allocations via the SportsBeep DAO.

4. Revenue & Sustainability

The SportsBeep ecosystem is built for long-term viability through multiple revenue streams that feed back into the Treasury and Reward pool:

  • Tournament Entry Fees: A small protocol fee (2.5% - 5%) per entry.
  • NFT Marketplace Royalties: Fees from secondary sales of player cards.
  • Brand Partnerships: Integrated sponsorships within the gaming interface.
  • Staking Commissions: A portion of rewards from the DAO treasury's validator nodes.

6. The Cardano Advantage

We chose Cardano for its security, scalability, and predictable transaction fees. By using Midnight for privacy and Hydra for scaling, SportsBeep can handle thousands of concurrent game entries with minimal latency.

  • Security: Formal verification of Plutus contracts.
  • Cost: Low, predictable fees regardless of network congestion.
  • Sustainability: Proof-of-Stake consensus aligns with our green tech values.
